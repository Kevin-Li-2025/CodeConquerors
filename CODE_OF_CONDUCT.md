# Code of Conduct

AccessCity welcomes technical collaboration that improves accessibility, safety, reliability, and engineering clarity.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat contributors and users with respect.
- Keep discussion focused on evidence, reproducible behavior, and concrete tradeoffs.
- Be direct about risks without personal attacks.
- Respect privacy, location sensitivity, accessibility needs, and security boundaries.
- Credit prior work, datasets, tools, and external research where relevant.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private credentials, personal data, location history, or security-sensitive details.
- Misrepresenting benchmark results, model accuracy, production readiness, or safety guarantees.
- Pressuring maintainers to bypass tests, security checks, data rights, or accessibility validation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate this policy. Repeated or severe violations may result in restricted participation.

Reports should be sent privately to the maintainers when possible, especially when they involve security, privacy, or personal safety.
